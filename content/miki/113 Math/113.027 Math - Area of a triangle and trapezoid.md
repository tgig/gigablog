# Area of a triangle and trapezoid

Area of a triangle
- $$Area = \frac{1}{2} * base * height$$


Area of a trapezoid
$$Area = h * (\frac{b1 + b2}{2})$$
- `b1` and `b2` are the heights of each base (the parallel sides of the trapezoid)
