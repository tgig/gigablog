# Total probability rule for expected value

- $$E(X) = E(X | S_1)P(S_1) + E(X | S_2)P(S_2) + ... + E(X | S_n)P(S_n)$$
- Where `S_1, S_2, ...` are mutually exclusive and exhaustive
