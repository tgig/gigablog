# Are you trying to escape feeling shamed or guilty?

When I examine the "why" behind my actions, and I discover that I'm trying to escape punishment, shame, or guilt, this is not something bad in-and-of-itself, but but it's important that I know it, am comfortable with the price I'm paying, and do it with choice.
